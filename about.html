About Gulshan Business Club (GBC)

Empowering Future Business Leaders

Gulshan Business Club (GBC) is a professional platform dedicated to leadership development, networking, entrepreneurship and innovation. We inspire students, entrepreneurs and professionals to connect, collaborate and grow together.
